Phuc Dao

Vietnamese-American who came to the United States at the age of 3. One of three boys in the Dao family. Grew up in East Price Hill and attended St. Lawrence Elementary School, Elder HS, and subsequently graduated from the University of Cincinnati Lidner College of Business. Began his career as a Case Manager at Ohio National Financial, moved to Cleveland for a short stint at N2 Publishing and coming back home to work at a local Allstate agency as a Licensed Sales Producer. He is one half of a wedding/lifestyle photography duo with soon-to-be wife, Vy Pham. Has a keen interest in photo composition and attention to detail in post processing. Current student at the Max Technical Training School in Mason, Ohio and seeking a fulfilling career in the world of computer programming.
